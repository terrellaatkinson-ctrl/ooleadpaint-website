What to Do Before Your Inspection

  • Clear Access: Ensure rooms and areas to be inspected are accessible
  • Document Issues: Make note of any peeling paint or areas of concern
  • Gather Info: Collect records of any renovations or repairs
  • Prepare Questions: Write down any questions about lead hazards
  • Schedule Time: Plan for 1‚Äì2 hours for the inspection
  • Have Adults Present: Someone should be available to discuss findings

Key Prevention Steps

  • Regular Cleaning: Use damp cloths and HEPA vacuum cleaners
  • Hand Washing: Wash hands frequently, especially before eating
  • Shoe Control: Remove shoes when entering from outdoors
  • Paint Maintenance: Keep lead paint in good condition; don't sand or scrape
  • Professional Help: Hire certified contractors for any renovations
  • Testing: Get children tested for lead exposure
  • Professional Assessment: Schedule a lead inspection with us
